Who we are

Clear Cut Digital is a team of highly skilled web developers, software engineers, and graphic designers. Our goal is to help small businesses and entrepeneurs set up an online presence with as few hurdles as possible.

We do this by prioritizing what we believe is the most important value in a digital agency: communication. We make sure to thoroughly understand your vision, and then to digitally express that vision in a timely fashion. We don't just talk about it, we mean it. That's why we offer a free consultation to all potential clients to make sure they have a platform to express their idea.

FAQ

What is the timeline for website creation?
Each website requires a unique design, functionality, and requirements. For that reason, an accurate timeline can only be provdied once we know the specific details and scope of your website. With that being said, the average website takes anywhere from 14-30 days.
Do I need to be tech savvy?
Not at all. If you were able to navigate to this website, and you can send a text message, then you're more than capable of owning your own website!
What platform do you use for building the websites?
We take pride in building custom websites from scratch using HTML5, CSS3, and Javascript. However, we are open to using WordPress for building websites that require easy integration will multiple applications. In additon, we can use page builders for clients who would like to manage and make changes to their sites on their own.
What languages do you offer service in?
We currently offer service in both English and French.
How do you charge for your web development service?
We generally charge in two installments. 50% to kickstart the project and the remaining 50% once you've approved the design of the project.
Do you offer website maintainence services?
Yes! We offer website maintainence services which includes, updating plugins or information, small fixes, and updating SEO. The rates vary based on which services are needed and the frequency of the maintainence.
